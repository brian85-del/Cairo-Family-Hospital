Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Care
Under One Roof

From routine checkups to advanced restorative and cosmetic procedures, our dental team at Cairo Family Hospital is equipped to care for every member of your family.

Dental Cleaning

Scaling removes plaque and tartar from above and below the gum line, while polishing smoothens teeth to reduce future buildup and enhance shine.

Includes: Professional scaling, polishing, fluoride treatment, and oral hygiene guidance.

Teeth Whitening

Professional bleaching treatment to lighten your existing tooth color by several shades, giving you a brighter, more confident smile.

Duration: 60-90 minutes | Results: Up to 8 shades lighter

Root Canal Treatment

Endodontic treatment to remove infected dental pulp and save your natural tooth — performed with care and precision by our experienced team.

Pain-free procedure with modern anesthesia | Saves natural tooth

Dental Fillings

Restoration of teeth damaged by decay back to normal function and shape using quality filling materials — fast, comfortable, and durable.

Materials: Composite resin, ceramic, or amalgam | Tooth-colored options

Dental Implants

Titanium implants surgically placed in the jawbone to replace missing tooth roots, topped with a crown that looks and feels like a natural tooth.

Long-lasting solution | Success rate: 95-98% | Natural look & feel

Dental Braces

Orthodontic appliances to straighten teeth and correct bite problems — applying gentle, controlled pressure over time for a perfect smile.

Options: Metal, ceramic, or lingual braces | Payment plans available

Pediatric Dentistry

Child-friendly dental care with a gentle touch — from first visits and cleanings to fillings and preventive treatments for growing smiles.

Ages: 1-18 | Fun, calm environment | Preventive sealants

Dentures

Custom-fitted removable or fixed dentures to replace missing teeth and restore functionality, comfort, and the natural appearance of your smile.

Full or partial dentures | Comfortable fit guaranteed

Crowns & Bridges

Custom dental crowns and bridges to restore damaged teeth or replace missing ones — durable, natural-looking, and precision-fitted.

Materials: Porcelain, zirconia, or ceramic | Same-day crowns available

About Our Dental Department

Dental Care That Puts
Your Family First

Cairo Family Hospital's Dental Department was established as part of our mission to provide safe, compassionate, and accessible healthcare for families in Ruiru and Kiambu County. We believe dental health is integral to overall wellbeing.

Our facility is fully licensed at Level 3B by the Ministry of Health and is led by Dr. Namu David Mbogo and a dedicated team of dental professionals committed to your family's oral health.
