About Our Paediatric Unit

Healthy Children,
Healthy Families

Our paediatric unit at Cairo Family Hospital offers a warm, child-friendly environment where the comfort and safety of your child come first. We understand that a sick child is stressful for the whole family — which is why our team is dedicated to putting both children and parents at ease from the moment you arrive.

Our paediatric clinic is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — walk-ins welcome, appointments available.
Comprehensive Care

What Our Paediatric Unit Offers

Complete healthcare services for children from newborns to adolescents.

Well-Child Checkups

Comprehensive developmental assessments & growth monitoring to ensure your child is thriving at every stage.

Immunization Programme

Full childhood vaccination schedules following Kenya's national guidelines — on-time protection for your little ones.

Inpatient Ward

Child-friendly environment with 24/7 nursing care, designed to make hospital stays comfortable for both child and family.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency response for acute childhood conditions — always ready when you need us most.

Neonatal Care

Specialized newborn monitoring, jaundice management, and early intervention services for the tiniest patients.

Growth Monitoring

Early identification of developmental delays with appropriate referrals and intervention planning.

What to Expect

We've designed every step of your child's visit to be as comfortable and stress-free as possible.

1

Warm Welcome

Our friendly front desk team will greet you and handle registration quickly so your child can be seen promptly.

2

Child-Friendly Assessment

Our paediatric team uses gentle, age-appropriate approaches to examine your child and put them at ease.

3

Clear Communication

We explain diagnoses, treatment options, and follow-up care in simple terms — and answer all your questions.

4

Ongoing Support

From prescriptions to specialist referrals if needed — we ensure continuity of care for your child.
