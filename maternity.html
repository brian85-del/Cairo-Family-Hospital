About Our Maternity Unit

A Safe Beginning for Every Family

At Cairo Family Hospital, we believe every mother deserves safe, respectful, and personalised care throughout her pregnancy journey. Our maternity unit is staffed by experienced clinicians and nurses who are committed to supporting you at every stage — from your very first antenatal visit to postnatal recovery.

Our maternity unit operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Emergency obstetric care and ambulance referrals are available at all times.
