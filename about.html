About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Ruiru & Kiambu County

Cairo Family Hospital Ruiru Ltd is a registered and licensed Level 3B Private Health Facility, incorporated on 12th June 2024. We are dedicated to delivering patient-centered, ethical, and affordable healthcare to the Ruiru and greater Kiambu community.

We bridge the gap between primary clinics and referral hospitals by offering high-quality clinical care with the warmth and accessibility of a family-centered hospital.

  • Fully licensed by the Ministry of Health and PPB
  • Comprehensive inpatient, outpatient & diagnostic services
  • Led by Dr. Namu David Mbogo, licensed Superintendent
  • Compliant with Medical Practitioners & Pharmacy Acts
  • SHA accepted — private insurer onboarding in progress
