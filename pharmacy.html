FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pharmacy services.

Can I order medicine without visiting the hospital? ▼
Yes! For over-the-counter (OTC) products, simply message us on WhatsApp with your request and we'll prepare your order for pickup or arrange local delivery. For prescription medicines, you'll need to share a valid prescription first.
Do you accept SHA or health insurance for pharmacy purchases? ▼
Yes, we accept SHA (Social Health Authority) and are in the process of onboarding select private insurers. Please inquire with our pharmacy team to confirm whether your specific insurance covers the medicines you need.
What are your pharmacy operating hours? ▼
Our pharmacy is open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. For urgent medication needs outside these hours, please call our emergency line at +254 758 833 863 and we'll do our best to assist you.
How do I know my prescription medicines are genuine? ▼
All our medicines are sourced exclusively from licensed and registered Kenyan pharmaceutical distributors. Our pharmacy is PPB licensed, and we maintain full supply chain documentation for every product we stock.
Can the pharmacist advise me on my medication? ▼
Absolutely. Our licensed pharmacist is always available to counsel you on proper dosage, potential side effects, food and drug interactions, and safe storage of your medicines — at no extra charge.
Do you offer chronic disease medication refills? ▼
Yes! For patients managing chronic conditions like hypertension, diabetes, or asthma, we offer convenient refill services. You can even book a telemedicine follow-up via BEYON8 and get your refill prescription sent digitally.
